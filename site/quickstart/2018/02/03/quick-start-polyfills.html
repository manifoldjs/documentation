Quick Start Polyfills

Given that not every platform or browser runs PWAs yet, the builder provides polyfills for platforms that lack support like iOS, Mac and Windows 7. In case of Android, PWAs are available through the browser; however, you can generate an Android polyfill app that can be also submitted to the Play store.

Android

  1. Follow the Quick Start PWA on Web o the Quick Start PWA using CLI tools to generate the polyfills for Android.
  2. Then, follow the instructions to publish your Android app.

iOS

  1. Follow the Quick Start PWA on Web o the Quick Start PWA using CLI tools to generate the polyfills for iOS.
  2. Then, follow the instructions to publish your iOS app.

MacOS

  1. Follow the instructions to publish your MacOS app.

Windows 7

  1. Follow the instructions to publish your Windows 7 app.
